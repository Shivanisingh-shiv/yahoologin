Yahoo Login Requirements

To access your Yahoo account, you'll need:

  • Your Yahoo ID, email address or mobile number
  • Your current Yahoo account password
  • Access to your recovery email or phone (for verification)
  • An internet connection
  • Supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) or Yahoo app
Note: Yahoo requires JavaScript to be enabled in your browser for the login page to work properly.

How to Login to Yahoo

1

Via Yahoo Homepage

Go to yahoo.com and click "Sign In" in the top right corner. Enter your Yahoo ID and password, then click "Next" to access your account.

2

Using Yahoo Mobile App

Download the Yahoo app from your device's app store. Open the app and tap "Sign In" to enter your credentials and access all Yahoo services.

3

Direct Service Login

Access specific services directly: mail.yahoo.com for Yahoo Mail, finance.yahoo.com for Yahoo Finance, etc. Your login works across all Yahoo properties.

Login Troubleshooting

Common Yahoo Login Issues

  • Forgotten Password: Use Yahoo's password recovery tool at login.yahoo.com/forgot
  • Account Locked: Wait 12-24 hours after multiple failed attempts or verify your identity
  • Browser Problems: Clear cookies/cache, disable extensions, or try a different browser
  • Two-Step Verification: Ensure you can access your recovery email or phone number
  • App Login Failure: Update the Yahoo app or reinstall it from the official app store
Security Alert: Always verify you're on an official Yahoo domain (yahoo.com or yahoo-inc.com) before entering your credentials. Yahoo will never ask for your password via email.
